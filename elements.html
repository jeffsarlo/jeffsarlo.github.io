My name is

Jeff Sarlo

An accomplished Financial Operations and Project Management professional with a successful track record of developing systems, streamlining operations, and profitably implementing business objectives. After leaving TD Ameritrade in 2018, I took a short break to contemplate the next stage of my career. Seeing it's potential impact on the finance industry, blockchain was a technology that I wanted to learn more about and to see it was an area where I could apply my skills. After reading a book on blockchain and understanding less than half of it's content (cryptography), I decided to join Blockgeeks.com, hoping their classes taught from a non-technical perspective. Initially they did, but I again hit a roadblock due to my lack of programming skills. Refusing to give up I decided to just put aside blockchain for the time being and start from the beginning, so I logged into Amazon and ordered my first beginner's guide to HTML & CSS...

...Fast forward a year an a half, six books, a dozen online courses, and countless tutorials later and I can proudly say that I am proficient with HTML, CSS, Javascript and React. I can effectively utilize Javascript/CSS libraries such as jQuery, Bootstrap, Materialize and I have a working knowledge of Node.js, MongoDB and Express.

There are many talented programmers out there today, but what sets me apart is my professional experience. I am a highly motivated person with a passion for creating autonomously or as part of a team. I have a natural ability for solving problems that was further refined through managing people and processes. Most importantly, my client-centric focus has helped me develop a strong understanding and ability to see through the eyes of end users. This I will use to my advantage when designing, creating or collaborating in this field.
